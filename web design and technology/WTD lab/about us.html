About Us

Welcome to Kiri Da Dhaba, your go-to destination for authentic Indian cuisine made with love and tradition. Established in 2020, our journey started with a passion to bring homemade flavors and street-style spices together in a cozy, rustic setting.

At Kiri Da Dhaba, we believe in using fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a whole lot of heart. Whether you're craving rich North Indian gravies, sizzling tandoori dishes, or South Indian delights, our menu offers something for everyone.

Join us for a warm meal, family-style hospitality, and the true taste of India. Thank you for being part of our story!
